untitled

send more gifts
write more notes
say I love you more often

as you or I

the whole universe in a room

sonnet

There will come a time for us, my dearest-
when the world is through with its turbulence
and when the moon whispers her forgiveness
and stars are all we need for vigilance.
There will be worlds meant for us, my darling-
worlds where distance melts as it does in dreams,
where we wake in each other's arms finding
ourselves no longer lost in any seas.

But when you hold me close to you, my dear-
the whole universe signs an armistice,
and the moon leans down through the atmosphere
to kiss the mountains full of promises.
And when in dreaming's end you do not dissapear,
we will have found the world that's meant for us.

Victoria Naylor
