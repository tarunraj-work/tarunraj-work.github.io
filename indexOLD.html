BEGINNINGS

The goddess Durga is the warrior goddess who fought and defeated the evil Mahishasura.
Durga's story begins with a simple demon named Mahishasura.
A shapeshifting demon with the head of a water buffalo, Mahishasura dreamt of power.
So to get this power, he prayed for an eon.
Eventually, the god of all gods, appeared to answer his prayer.
"I wish to get the boon myself to make it more powerful. I wish that I cannot be killed by any man, God or animal. I wish to be the most powerful of all creatures ever created," Mahishasura immediately said.
The greater being had no choice but to respect the demon's penance, and granted his wish.

DESTRUCTION

Mahishasura returned home a triumphant demon and began preparations for war to expand his kingdom.
Empowered by his boon, no one was able to stand up against Mahishasura and soon he became powerful.
He ruled all over the earth.
However Mahishasura just did not conquer kingdoms. He destroyed the kingdoms, and terrorized the people.
Anyone who stood up against Mahishasura was mercilessly killed by the demon.
Meanwhile Mahishasura set his sight on the heavens.
He invaded the heavens and defeated Indra, the king of the Gods.
